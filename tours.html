Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our tour packages and visa services.

The required documents typically include a valid passport, completed visa application form, passport-sized photos, proof of accommodation, travel itinerary, proof of funds, travel insurance, and sometimes an invitation letter. Requirements vary by country, so we provide a personalized checklist for each application.

Processing times vary by country and visa type. Tourist visas typically take 2-4 weeks, student visas 4-8 weeks, and work permits 1-3 months. We expedite where possible and keep you updated throughout the process.

Some of our packages include flights, while others are land-only. Each package description clearly states what's included. We can also customize any package to include or exclude flights based on your preferences.

Our standard policy offers full refunds for cancellations made 30+ days before departure, 50% refund for 15-29 days, and no refund for less than 15 days. Travel insurance is recommended and available through us. Special packages may have different terms clearly outlined at booking.

Still have questions? Contact our team for personalized assistance.
