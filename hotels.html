Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our hotel booking service

How does your hotel booking service work?

Simply fill out our quote form with your accommodation needs and preferences. Our travel experts will research the best options for you, negotiate rates with our hotel partners, and present you with personalized recommendations within 2 hours. Once you approve, we handle all the booking details for you.

How quickly will I receive my hotel recommendations?

We typically respond within 2 hours during business hours with a curated list of hotel options that match your criteria and budget. For urgent bookings, you can call us directly and we'll prioritize your request.

Do you really guarantee the best rates?

Yes! Thanks to our partnerships with hotels and our local expertise, we often secure rates that aren't available to the public. If you find a lower rate elsewhere for the same hotel and dates, we'll match it and provide additional value through our service.

Can I make changes to my booking after it's confirmed?

Yes, we handle all booking modifications for you. Whether you need to change dates, room types, or other preferences, just contact us and we'll work with the hotel to accommodate your changes (subject to hotel policies and availability).

What payment methods do you accept and when do I pay?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and cash payments. Payment is typically required upon booking confirmation, though we can arrange payment plans for longer stays or group bookings. No payment is required for the initial quote request.

Do you provide support during my stay?

Absolutely! We provide 24/7 support during your stay. If any issues arise with your accommodation, just contact us and we'll work directly with the hotel to resolve them. We're your advocate throughout your entire travel experience.

Can you help with other travel arrangements?

Yes! We can arrange airport transfers, local tours, car rentals, and travel insurance as part of your accommodation package. Our goal is to provide a complete travel solution that saves you time and ensures a seamless experience.
